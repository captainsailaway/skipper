  • Send dates + crew size (and your preferred start area).

FAQ

Quick answers guests usually ask.

Do we need sailing experience?

No. Guests don’t need experience. You enjoy the trip; I handle the boat.

Can you help us book the yacht?

Yes — I can propose suitable options from charter partners and help you choose the best one for your budget.

Where do we start — Zadar, Šibenik or Split?

Šibenik is great for Kornati and calmer logistics. Zadar is perfect for a northern route. Split is best for Hvar/Vis (if you prefer south).

What if the weather is bad?

We adjust the route and choose safer legs and protected bays. Safety always comes first.
